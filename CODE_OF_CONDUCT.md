# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:
- Respectful communication
- Accepting constructive criticism
- Supporting others

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment or discrimination
- Insults or disrespectful comments

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate corrective action.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces and interactions related to this project.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to the maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Maintainers will follow appropriate guidelines to determine consequences for unacceptable behavior.
